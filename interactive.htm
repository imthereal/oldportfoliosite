Interactive Stories

Concept & Design
Project Overview
What if there were interactive components that tell Facebook story viewers about the location taken, friends tagged, and music playing in a story? How would this look from the viewer's perspective?
My Contributions
This was a solo project. I created the product mockups in Sketch, with some use of internal Facebook plug-ins.
To start, I audited the various press-and-hold interactions within the Snapchat ecosystem. Then, I had some crude conversations with friends and reflected on the existing Facebook Stories platform.
‚Äç
I learned that Facebook Stories is more focused on sharing content and experiences, whereas Snapchat is more focused on conversational back-and-forth or blasting quick updates to friends. To provide more context for a given experience presented in a Story, it made sense to add a location feature that shows where a story was recorded.

I also learned that Facebook is much more focused on interactions with people within a social network, rather than individual people that you know. I added the option to view tagged Facebook users within a story due to the network-based nature of the platform.

I also added a music finder feature that scans the music playing within a given Story. This is more of a "nice to have," as I often find myself wondering what music is playing in video content.
